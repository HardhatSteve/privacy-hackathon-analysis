## Video Format Observations

From analyzing available videos:

1. **Platform**: 85% use Mux streaming, 15% use YouTube
2. **Length**: Typically 2-4 minutes
3. **Structure**: Hook → Problem → Demo → Tech Stack → CTA
4. **Quality**: 720p minimum, most are 1080p
5. **Recent trend**: Bangkok/Singapore 2024 have near-universal video submissions

## Direct Download Links

All Mux videos can be downloaded directly by appending `/high.mp4` to the stream URL:
```
https://stream.mux.com/{VIDEO_ID}/high.mp4
```

Alternative quality options:
- `/medium.mp4` - 720p
- `/low.mp4` - 480p
